ABOUT US

1. What is this?

PODCAST ON MARS is a Discord server that anyone can join.

2. What can i do there?

You can just come and hang out. You can listen to music, watch a movie, do podcasting, talk with others and post some memes or your story.
You can even play games with each other.

3. Various roles

Roles could be granted to any member (All are podcasters and member of abandon shack basically). And above other roles can be granted depending on your interests.

4. how can i join to the server?

If you got a code from the moderator, You can click "JOIN" button on the main page.

Feel free to join our cool server!
